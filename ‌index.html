— Who I Am

Scientist.
Educator. Innovator.

A physicist by training and an AI specialist by practice, I hold a Master of Science in Physics from Eastern Kentucky University — a foundation built on analytical precision, experimental rigour, and deep problem-solving.

At Scale AI, I've spent years at the cutting edge of large language model development — designing adversarial prompts to stress-test reasoning, conducting comparative evaluations using detailed rubrics, and shaping models toward more natural, human-like responses. My work spans major initiatives including Mail Valley and White Wolf.

In parallel, I continue my research at Morehead State University, collaborating with faculty on physics-based projects spanning data analysis and theoretical modelling. My career is defined by one constant: applying scientific rigour to complex, evolving systems.
